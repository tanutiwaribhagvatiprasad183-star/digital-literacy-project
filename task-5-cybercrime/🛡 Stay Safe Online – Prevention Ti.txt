🛡 Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips
Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone
Always verify the UPI ID before making payments
Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
Do not accept payment requests from unknown people
Use only official and trusted apps for transactions
Enable app lock and phone security
Regularly check your bank transaction history
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
